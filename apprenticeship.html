Software Apprenticeship: Class of 2016

Introduction

While talking with Simone Kamer about a career switch I started to think about things to teach somebody new to the profession of a Software Engineer.
She was interested to learn and I'm enthousiastic about teaching and coaching, so a Software Apprenticeship was the logical next step.

When the Software Apprenticeship really started I wondered if my brother-in-law Sjoerd van Houten might be interested as well. One text message later the class of 2016> had it's second student.

Approach

We agreed to meet one evening each week, where I would explain some stuff related to software engineering and practice something together
On November 22nd of 2016 we started with our lessons
After each lesson they got some homework as well to try it on their own and expand their knowledge and learning.

Keeping track

Initially I hoped to keep track of all lessons and topics, which I taught my students.
Unfortunately preparations for the lessons, the evenings themselves and my normal job and live, prevented my from doing that properly.

Update: Class dismissed

  1. On 2018-01-01 Sjoerd van Houten got a job as a Software Engineer at Ximedes
  2. On 2019-01-01 Simone Kamer got a job as a Junior Developer at Incura
